What I do?

This is Me

Motion graphics

A motion graphics editor is a professional who specializes in creating animated graphics and visual effects for various media platforms, such as film, television, advertising, and digital media. They use software tools like Adobe After Effects, Cinema 4D, and Maya to design and animate visual elements such as text, logos, and 3D models.

Photography and editing

A photographer and editor is a professional who specializes in capturing and editing photographs for various media platforms such as advertising, social media, print media, and other digital media. They use their technical skills and artistic vision to capture and edit images that convey a specific message or tell a story

Flyers

I am professional who specializes in creating visually appealing flyers and other promotional materials for various purposes, such as advertising events, promoting businesses, and showcasing products and services. I use graphic design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign to create and edit images, text, and other graphic elements.

Video Editing

As a video editor is a professional, specialized in assembling, manipulating, and fine-tuning raw video footage into a polished final product for various media platforms, such as film, television, advertising, social media, and digital media. They use video editing software such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and Avid Media Composer to create seamless, engaging, and impactful video content.
